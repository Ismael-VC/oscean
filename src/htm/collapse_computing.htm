Four Concepts Of Resilience
Agility Agility is the ability is the capacity to adapt or respond rapidly to a changing environment.
Preparedness Preparedness is the ability to reflect on past threats, and bouncing forward by enacting new ideas for development after the crisis event.
Elasticity Elasticity means increasing the exchangeability and flexibility of relationships among people and things within an organization and a wider ecosystem
Redundancy Redundancy is the intentional duplication of critical components of a system with the goal of increasing reliability of a system.
