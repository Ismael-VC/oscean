    Dexe is a hex editor.

    Dexe is a graphical hex editor created to inspect and edit binary files, it displays bytes, ascii characters as well as sprites in the .chr format.

    It is currently used in the creation of Famicom and Uxn projects such as spacetime, the 1bit illustrations of the Neauismetica and the interface design of all the ecosystem tools, including Orca.
