    Notes and links related to the Macintosh II computer.

    The Macintosh II computer used the excellent OS7 operating system, supporting great software such as Hypercard, and THINK Pascal.

    I used the Macintosh ecosystem to illustrate parts of the Neauismetica project, the idea of virtualizing an entire workstation has inspired the Uxn project, which is now my main virtual workstation.
