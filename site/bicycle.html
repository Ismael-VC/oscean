    Bicycle is an interactive Uxntal playground.

    Bicycle is a little environment to learn and experiment with Varvara. It's designed to allow someone to write small programs in a non-destructive setup, and iterate on routines while being able to see the state of the computer change in real-time.

    Bicycle includes the Drifblim assembler.
