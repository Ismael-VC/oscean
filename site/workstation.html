Technical details on my computer setups.

The following setups are geared toward audio & visual development. When living at anchor, power is a critical resource aboard Pino, I prefer to work on a low-power SBC like Usagi otherwise my main workstation is ayatori.

My entire workstation exists within the portable Varvara virtual-machine, each software is written in assembly and fits within 64kb of space. I also occasionally use an emulated Macintosh for various Pascal projects. To learn more about that audio equipment, see the studio.
