    Marabu is a music tool.

    Marabu was a tracker-type composition tool and synthesizer, it was original built as a fork to the opensource Soundbox, and is meant to be an improvement on functionalities and design. Marabu advised the creation of the Orca livecoding environment, and Uxnemu's APU.
