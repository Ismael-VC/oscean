    Manifest files are the application definition standard for Varvara software.

    The location of a rom's metadata is located at the address specified at location #0001 in memory.

    |0100
	;meta #f0 DEO
BRK

    The metadata begins with the address where the metadata ends, followed by the address of the appicon, a 8x8 icon, and four zero-terminated strings:

    @meta =&end =appicon
	( name ) "Catclock $1
	( version ) "Ver. 20 "4 $1
	( details ) "tic 20 "tac 20 "cat 20 "clock $1
	( author ) "Hundred 20 "Rabbits 20 7f 20 "2022 $2
	&end

    A manifest.tal is a support library that populates a menubar with the application's shortcuts. You can see support coverage here, it is also used in the definition of applications in Potato.

    @manifest
	04 "Application $1
		01 'n :file-new "New $1
		01 'r :file-rename "Rename $1
		01 'o :file-open "Open $1
		01 's :file-save "Save $1
	03 "Edit $1
		01 'c :edit-copy "Copy $1
		01 'v :edit-paste "Paste $1
		01 'x :edit-cut "Cut $1
	02 "Select $1
		00 1b :select-reset "Reset $1
		01 'a :select-all "All $1
	$1
