    Drifblim is a Uxntal assembler.

    The assembler is written in Uxntal itself, it's designed to be distributed as a rom to help bootstrap a uxn ecosystem. The goal would be to have its raw hex codes printed on a single piece of paper, it should also be available as a monochromatic picture where each pixel is a bit. It might be important or not to also generate the symbols file from the assembler, the option is available to do so in the respository.
