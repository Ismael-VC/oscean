    Symbol files are debug information about an assembled rom.

    A symbols(*.sym) file contains a series of absolute addresses(2 bytes) and null-terminated strings. They are generated by Uxntal assemblers, such as Drifblim, and are used by debugging tools, such as Beetbug, which do not otherwise have access to a program's label.
